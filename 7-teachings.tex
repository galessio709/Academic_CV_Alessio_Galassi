\section{Teachings}

    \begin{onecolentry}
        \textbf{Object-Centric Process Mining \& Blockchain} - Six-hour lectures for master's students on Process Mining, covering object-centric process mining, its evolution, and application to blockchain event logs. Included discussion on representing smart contract data as object-centric event logs and verifying compliance in DApps usage.
    \end{onecolentry}

    \begin{onecolentry}
        \textbf{Process Intelligence - INGKA Group \@Unicam} - Two-hour seminar for master's students on Process Mining, presenting a practical application in inventory management. Covered compliance checking approaches, methodologies for large-scale industrial projects, and results from the \textit{UNICAM 4 INGKA Inventory Process Mining} project.
    \end{onecolentry}
